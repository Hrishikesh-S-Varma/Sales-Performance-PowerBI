## Project Overview
An advanced Power BI dashboard analyzing football player performance across top European leagues using detailed statistical metrics.

## Business Objective
To assess player strengths, weaknesses, and overall impact for scouting, coaching, and performance evaluation.

## Key KPIs
- Goals, Assists, xG
- Key Passes & Shot-Creating Actions
- Defensive Contributions & Duels
- On-Off Impact Metrics
- Progressive Play Indicators

## Dashboard Pages
1. Player Snapshot  
2. Attacking Performance  
3. Passing & Creativity  
4. Defensive Contribution & Duels  
5. Playing Impact & Weakness Detection  

## Tools Used
- Power BI
- Power Query
- DAX
- FBref Data
- GitHub

## Outcome
The dashboard provides a holistic and data-driven evaluation of football player performance beyond traditional statistics.
